Bio

Hello. I'm a Ph.D. student in Robotics at CU Boulder with a focus on motion planning and coordinating multi-agent teams. My research focuses on developing efficient algorithms with formal guarantees for reliable planning in complex environments.

My background spans robotics, machine learning, aerospace and mechanical engineering, with hands-on experience building full autonomy stacks and implementing planning algorithms on real robotic systems. I've previously worked at NASA's IV&V Facility and founded Sneaker Space, a software-driven e-commerce business.
